Pagespeedy

Pagespeedy is an automated thingymabob by jumoo, that scans a load of websites, runs some tests and tries to work some stuff out.

localgov.pagespeedy

the localgov version of Pagespeedy, does all that against a list of over 400 local council sites.

Running at the start of every month, localgov pagespeedy - gets a google pagespeed insight score for a site, runs the homepage through an accessibility checker, and runs a slightly modified version of wappalyzer to work out a best guess at what the site is running.
